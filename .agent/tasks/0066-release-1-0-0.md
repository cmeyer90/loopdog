## Decisions

## Risks / Rollback

- **Shipping with a hidden V1 gap** (a loop/backend/adapter that fails on a
  foreign repo) â€” mitigated by the readiness gate being grounded in the *real*
  0063 dogfood, not internal tests; any failed line blocks the release.
- **Premature `1.0.0`** committing a compatibility promise too early â€” mitigated
  by gating on 0064/0065 and enumerating the breaking-change baseline; 0067 owns
  the forward upgrade contract.
- **Botched/partial publish** â€” re-run is idempotent (0005); manual `npm publish`
  + `git tag` fallback is documented. Rollback of a bad `1.0.0`: `npm deprecate`
  `@looper/cli@1.0.0`, ship `1.0.1` with the fix; do not unpublish past the
  window or reuse the version.
